✂️ SnippingTool
Features

Capture & Record like a Pro

A modern, lightning-fast screen capture and recording tool for Windows. Record your screen, add live annotations to explain your point, and redact sensitive data on the fly.

Download for Windows
$ winget install DimitarRadenkov.SnippingTool
Click to copy

Why use this over the built-in Windows tool?

Built for speed, privacy, and seamless communication.

Live Video Annotations

Draw, highlight, and point things out while you are recording. No need for complicated post-production video editing to explain a bug or tutorial.

Privacy First (Live Blur)

Drag over sensitive content (passwords, emails, API keys) to apply a Gaussian blur instantly. It stays blurred even in your exported video recordings.

Built-in OCR (Text Extraction)

Can't copy text from an image or video? Just lasso the text on your screen with SnippingTool, and it instantly extracts and copies it to your clipboard.

Pin to Screen

Working on something complex? Pin a captured screenshot as a floating, always-on-top window for quick reference while you code or type.
